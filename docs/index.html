Water Conservation

Our school water conservation plan focuses on protecting our
coastal environment by reducing waste and preventing ocean
pollution. We will install rainwater harvesting barrels to
collect irrigation water and replace thirsty lawns with native
coastal plants that thrive in sandy soil without extra watering.

To prevent saltwater intrusion into our local supply, we’ll use
smart sensors to stop over-pumping and install permeable
pathways that let rain soak into the ground. Finally, we will
stencil "Only Rain Down the Drain" on outdoor pipes to ensure
our CORE values protect the marine life right in our backyard.
